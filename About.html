My Journey with Motorcycles

My love affair with motorcycles began at a young age, watching my father work on his racing bikes in our garage. What started as curiosity soon became an obsession – not just with riding, but with understanding the engineering that makes these machines come alive.

I pursued degrees in both industrial engineering and international business, knowing I wanted to combine technical expertise with global perspective. This unique blend allowed me to see opportunities where others saw challenges – particularly in bringing exceptional motorcycles like the Kawasaki Ninja H2 to enthusiasts worldwide.

YoruTouge Moto isn't just a business – it's an extension of my passion. The name (combining "Yoru" for night and "Touge" for Japan's legendary mountain passes) reflects the thrill of riding under the stars, pushing both machine and skill to their limits. Each custom build we create is a reflection of this philosophy – blending performance with personality.

"A motorcycle isn't just transportation – it's a statement. It's about the connection between rider and machine, the perfect balance of power and control. That's what we celebrate at YoruTouge."
